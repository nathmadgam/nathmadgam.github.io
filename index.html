As a kid, I was obsessed with playing games and figured out I could build my own. I grew tired of repetitive gameplay loops, so around 8 years ago I started learning programming inside Roblox Studio. What started as curiosity turned into a craft.

I am a fullstack developer and a computer science student currently specializing in Game Development at AMA University. Before that I graduated senior high school in Accounting, Business and Management at FEU Alabang, which also shaped how I think about the business side of games and studios.

I speak English, Japanese, Spanish, German, and Indonesian. I love learning, building things people actually enjoy, and solving interesting problems.
